Career

Snoop Dogg's Career Summary

Snoop Dogg was discovered by Dr. Dre in the early 1990s. He began his musical career by playing piano and singing at his local Baptist Church. Snoopy (his childhood nickname) started rapping in 6th grade and recorded a demo that Dr. Dre soon found. Dr. Dre collaborted with him on a song called Deep Cover that made Snoop Dogg a household name. Snoop's first album DoggyStyle became the number one spot on Billboards hip-hop with the help of Dr. Dre!
